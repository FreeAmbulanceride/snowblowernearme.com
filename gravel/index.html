FAQ: Gravel driveways & snow blowers

Use two-stage or three-stage only. Set skid shoes high early-season; lower as the base packs.

Can I use single-stage on gravel?

Not recommended—rubber paddles can grab and throw stones.

What setup prevents rock pickup?

Adjust skid shoes so the intake sits above loose gravel; clear in layers.
